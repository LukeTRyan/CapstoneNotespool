Frequently Asked Questions

I can’t sign up using my email address?

Ensure you are using your student email address. If this still doesn’t work your university may not yet be supported. Please send us an email at opportunities@notespool.com to request access for your university.

I’ve made an account but I can’t find any of my units / I can find my units but I can’t join them?

Your university's representative may have yet to create your class’ unit / You may not have the correct permissions to join this Unit. Contact the relevant staff member at your university.

How do I upload documents to my unit page?

Navigate to Home > [Unit Name]. Then select ‘Add Notes’ in the appropriate section.

How can I report someone in my unit?

The Unit Admin or University Staff should be contacted in the case of misconduct. Please Navigate to Contact Us for more information.

How do I amend incorrect notes?

You can edit content directly or contact the Unit Admin or University Staff if they are disruptive or intentional.
