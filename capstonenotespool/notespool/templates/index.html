Welcome to Capstone Notespool

The Capstone Notespool is a project designed to help students share their notes outside of classes in an easy way. While this platform has been approved by QUT, it is not run by QUT. This means that the content provided here is only as good as the content you create!

We encourage Lecturers and Tutors to contribute and moderate where possible, but ultimately it is up to you and your fellow students to create, upload and curate content.
To access the content on Capstone Notespool, you will need to be logged in with your account. If you don't already have one, you can register with your QUT student email, or log in via Facebook.
